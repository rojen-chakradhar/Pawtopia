about us

Pawtopia is a non-profit dog welfare organisation established in 2025 A.D in the Bhaktapur district of Nepal. Our primary mission revolves around safeguarding dogs from any kind of mistreatment, cruelty, or inhuman treatment. We strive to protect them from all forms of abuse and suffering. Similarly, we provide medical care and shelter to injured, sick, and abandoned dogs in the community. We primarily focus on community dogs in need.


Our ethical principles revolves around love, compassion, kindness, dignity, and respect for dogs. We ensure the well-being of dogs through responsible actions and thoughtful care. For this to happen, we have broadened our area of work from the rescue and rehabilitation of community dogs to dog population management as well. We work to ensure the welfare of dogs. Our working area also incorporates helping dogs in disasters, ensuring their safety. We have also been advocating for the humane live dog transportation and veganism promotion.


Currently, our shelter has been home to more than 200 dogs.

our mission

Pawtopia passionately rescues, rehabilitates, and advocates for the welfare of diverse animals while promoting a plant-based lifestyle for a harmonious coexistence between humans and animals.

our vission

We envision a world where dogs are treated with kindness, compassion, dignity, and respect. We dream of a sustainable community, where plant-based alternatives are chosen instead of relying on animal-based products for food.
